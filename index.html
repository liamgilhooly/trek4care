Welcome to my Trek4Care website!

Welcome to Trek4Care, a website created to help me achieve my fundraising goals for Bradford Marie Curie and to help in a small way those who may be going through a similar experience Cancer is a terrible disease that touches so many lives, it knows no prejudice nor does it discrimate. perhaps it is the reason you have found my site, it is certainly the reason I am here and why I doing all I can for marie curie. read why

In December 2013 I lost my mum to Cancer of the Vulva, a terrible disease that took my mum at just 67 years old. For the last 2 months or so of my mum’s life she was cared for by the amazing Bradford Marie Curie Cancer Care unit. Their dedication, expertise, care and sensitivity to not just my mum, but to me and my entire family, was nothing short of incredible.

It costs £9,000 a day to run the unit…..a truly staggering amount, and I have made it my lifes ambition to raise as much money as possible to repay them and help them to continue their amazing work.
